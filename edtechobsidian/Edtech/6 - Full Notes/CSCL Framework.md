## Summary
Computer-Supported Collaborative Learning (CSCL) integrates tech to enhance group learning. Fanguy et al. (2023) exemplify this with forums and note-taking.

## Key Points
- **Tech Role:** Facilitates collaboration (Koehler et al., 2013).
- **Interaction:** Online tools boost engagement (Fanguy et al., 2023).
- **Benefits:** Shared effort, feedback loops.
- **Challenges:** Needs pedagogical design.

## Concepts
- **TPACK:** Tech, pedagogy, content synergy.
- **Collaborative Dynamics:** Group processes matter.
