# Social Interaction In Education

- **ID:** 20231125B
- **Source:** Derived from Ilham (2023) and Fahri & Qusyairi (2019).
- **Tags:** #SocialInteraction #EducationTheory #StudentDevelopment
- **Date:** 2025-03-25

## Summary
Social interaction is vital in education, fostering cognitive and social growth through dynamic relationships. Ilham (2023) shows its reduction in online learning impacts quality.

## Key Points
- **Role:** Drives learning via educator-student, peer interactions (Fahri & Qusyairi, 2019).
- **Impact:** Less interaction lowers comprehension (Ilham, 2023).
- **Theory:** Humans as social beings need interaction (Soekanto, 1982).
- **Context:** Shifts to cyberspace in online settings.

## Concepts
- **Reciprocity:** Action-reaction in learning.
- **Social Development:** Interaction shapes skills, character.
