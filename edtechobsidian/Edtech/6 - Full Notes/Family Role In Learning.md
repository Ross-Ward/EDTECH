## Summary
Family plays a supportive role in learning, amplified in online settings. Ilham (2023) notes stronger parent-child bonds during online learning.

## Key Points
- **Support:** More interaction aids moral growth (Ilham, 2023).
- **Understanding:** Frequent contact fosters empathy.
- **Context:** Home-based learning increases family time.
- **Benefit:** Offsets educational interaction loss.

## Concepts
- **Parental Involvement:** Shapes child development.
- **Home Environment:** Learning context shift.
