## Summary
Visual learning enhances comprehension and engagement. Cavaliero et al. (n.d.) show its impact via graphic facilitation.

## Key Points
- **Comprehension:** Images clarify ideas (Bell & Morse, 2012).
- **Engagement:** Boosts interest (Cavaliero et al., n.d.).
- **Creativity:** Sparks problem-solving.
- **Wellbeing:** Reduces anxiety (Bell & Robbins, 2007).

## Concepts
- **Visual Processing:** Brain favors images.
- **Situational Interest:** Visuals motivate.
