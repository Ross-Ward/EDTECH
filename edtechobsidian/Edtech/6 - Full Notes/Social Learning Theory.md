## Summary
Social Learning Theory posits learning occurs via observation and interaction. Ilham (2023) implies its relevance to online interaction challenges.

## Key Points
- **Observation:** Learning from others’ actions.
- **Interaction:** Key to development (Ilham, 2023).
- **Theory:** Bandura’s emphasis on social context.
- **Interaction:** Peer learning drives growth (Cavaliero et al., n.d.). 
- **Observation:** Modeling behaviors. 
- **Theory:** Bandura’s social context focus. 
- **Outcome:** Builds community.
## Concepts
- **Modeling:** Behavior imitation.
- **Vicarious Learning:** Indirect experience.
