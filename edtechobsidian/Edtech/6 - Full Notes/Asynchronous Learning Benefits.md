## Summary
Asynchronous learning allows flexible, self-paced education via online tools. Fanguy et al. (2023) show its role in flipped collaboration.

## Key Points
- **Flexibility:** Anytime access (Fanguy et al., 2023).
- **Reflection:** Time for deeper thought (Bikowski & Vithanage, 2016).
- **Collaboration:** Forums, notes thrive asynchronously.
- **Inclusivity:** Suits varied schedules.

## Concepts
- **Time Independence:** No fixed schedule.
- **Self-Directed Learning:** Student-driven pace.
