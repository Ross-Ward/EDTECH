# Scaffolding in Learning

- **ID:** 20230325I
- **Source:** Based on Vygotsky (1978) and Fanguy et al. (2023).
- **Tags:** #Scaffolding #CollaborativeLearning #EducationTheory
- **Date:** 2025-03-25

## Summary
Scaffolding involves support from experts or peers to help learners achieve independence. Fanguy et al. (2023) show it in collaborative writing.

## Key Points
- **Guidance:** Experts aid novices (Vygotsky, 1978).
- **Collaboration:** Peers scaffold each other (Storch, 2018).
- **Evidence:** Forums provide scaffolding, boosting scores (Fanguy et al., 2023).
- **Goal:** Independent mastery.

## Concepts
- **ZPD:** Learning within reachable limits.
- **Temporary Support:** Fades as skills grow.
