# Flexible Learning Environments

- **ID:** 20231125F
- **Source:** Derived from Ilham (2023) and Fahri & Qusyairi (2019).
- **Tags:** #FlexibleLearning #OnlineLearning #EducationTechnology
- **Date:** 2025-03-25

## Summary
Flexible learning environments offer time and place adaptability in online education. Ilham (2023) notes this as a benefit despite interaction challenges.

## Key Points
- **Flexibility:** Study anytime, anywhere (Ilham, 2023).
- **Access:** Broadens resource availability (Fahri & Qusyairi, 2019).
- **Benefit:** Suits diverse schedules.
- **Trade-off:** Less social interaction.

## Concepts
- **Asynchronous Learning:** Non-fixed timing.
- **Open Education:** Unrestricted access to materials.
