# Digital Distractions

- **ID:** 20231125E

## Summary
Digital distractions, like online games, divert student focus in online learning. Ilham (2023) identifies this as a key issue reducing educational quality.

## Key Points
- **Gaming:** Students prioritize games over learning (Ilham, 2023).
- **Impact:** Lowers comprehension, motivation.
- **Context:** Easy access via gadgets during online sessions.
- **Challenge:** Competes with educational content.

## Concepts
- **Attention Economy:** Digital media vies for focus.
- **Self-Regulation:** Student ability to resist distractions.
