# E-tivities in Online Learning

- **ID:** 20240325U
- **Source:** Based on Cavaliero et al. (n.d.) and Salmon (2013).
- **Tags:** #Etivities #OnlineLearning #EducationTechnology
- **Date:** 2025-03-25

## Summary
E-tivities structure online engagement. Cavaliero et al. (n.d.) use them to prepare staff for graphic facilitation classes.

## Key Points
- **Structure:** Prepares, connects learners (Salmon, 2013).
- **Engagement:** Boosts participation (Cavaliero et al., n.d.).
- **Peer Review:** Encourages feedback.
- **Benefit:** Builds confidence.

## Concepts
- **Scaffolding:** Gradual skill-building.
- **Asynchronous Interaction:** Forum-based.
