## Summary
Adaptive learning systems use AI to dynamically adjust educational content to individual needs. Thimmanna et al. (2024) highlight their role in personalized learning paths.

## Key Points
- **Dynamic Adjustment:** Tailors difficulty, pace (VanLehn, 2011).
- **Outcomes:** Boosts retention, engagement (Thimmanna et al., 2024).
- **Features:** Real-time feedback, customization.
- **Use Case:** Supports diverse learners.

## Concepts
- **Feedback Loops:** Continuous improvement.
- **Personalization Algorithms:** Match content to learner profiles.
