## Summary
AI in education raises ethical issues like privacy, bias, and equity. Thimmanna et al. (2024) note these as critical for responsible implementation.

## Key Points
- **Privacy:** Protecting student data (Williamson, 2017).
- **Bias:** Algorithmic fairness concerns.
- **Equity:** Ensuring inclusivity (Thimmanna et al., 2024).
- **Transparency:** Need for clear AI processes.

## Concepts
- **Data Ethics:** Responsible use of learner info.
- **Algorithmic Accountability:** Mitigating unintended outcomes.
