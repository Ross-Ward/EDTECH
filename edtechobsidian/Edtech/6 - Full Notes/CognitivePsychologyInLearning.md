## Summary
Cognitive psychology informs learning by aligning education with mental processes and styles. Thimmanna et al. (2024) use it to ground AI-driven personalization.

## Key Points
- **Learning Styles:** Visual, auditory, kinesthetic catered to (Pashler et al., 2008).
- **Outcomes:** Enhanced comprehension (Thimmanna et al., 2024).
- **Adaptation:** Matches cognitive preferences.
- **Theory:** Learning as mental construction.

## Concepts
- **Information Processing:** How learners absorb data.
- **Cognitive Load:** Balancing complexity for retention.
