## Summary
Student-centered learning prioritizes individual needs and pacing over teacher-led instruction. It aligns with flipped classrooms (Fanguy et al., 2023).

## Key Points
- **Personalization:** Tailored learning paths (Wanner & Palmer, 2015).
- **Autonomy:** Students control pace (J. Y. Kim, 2017).
- **Collaboration:** Peer support enhances outcomes (Fanguy et al., 2023).
- **Flexibility:** Adapts to diverse learners.

## Concepts
- **Self-Paced Learning:** Online prep in flipped models.
- **Agency:** Students drive their education.
