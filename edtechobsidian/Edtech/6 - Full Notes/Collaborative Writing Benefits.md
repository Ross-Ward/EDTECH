Collaborative writing improves skills via co-authorship. Fanguy et al. (2023) show its impact in flipped learning.

## Key Points
- **Understanding:** Deeper grasp (Fung, 2010).
- **Skills:** Critical thinking (Fanguy et al., 2023).
- **Perspectives:** Diverse views.

## Concepts

- **Reflection:** Asynchronous revisions.
