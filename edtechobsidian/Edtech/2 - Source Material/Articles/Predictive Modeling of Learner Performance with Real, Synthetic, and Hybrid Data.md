## üí° Synthesized Key Insights

The study shows hybrid datasets (real + synthetic) outperform real (330 records) and synthetic (5,000 records) datasets alone, with SVR achieving 87.76% accuracy on hybrid data. Diverse feature sets (P1‚ÄìP5) reduce RMSE (e.g., 12.17 for quad sets), confirming RQ1. Synthetic data via ACTGAN matches real data utility (RQ2), offering a privacy-compliant alternative. Limitations include regional specificity and potential synthetic data gaps.
