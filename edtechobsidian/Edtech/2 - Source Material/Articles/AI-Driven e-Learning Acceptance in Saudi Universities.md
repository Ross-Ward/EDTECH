## üéØ Multidimensional Research Inquiry

### Core Research Question
How do AI-driven e-learning platform characteristics and student traits influence acceptance and satisfaction among Saudi university students?

### Nested Research Sub-Questions
1. **Cognitive Dimension**: How do perceived usefulness (PU) and ease of use (PEOU) shape satisfaction and attitudes?
2. **Technological Intervention**: What roles do social learning networks, personal learning portfolios, and environments play in platform acceptance?
3. **Learning Ecosystem Impact**: How do self-efficacy, innovativeness, and readiness moderate e-learning intentions?
