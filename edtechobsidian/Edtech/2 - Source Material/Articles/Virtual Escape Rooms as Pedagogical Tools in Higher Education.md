## üéØ Multidimensional Research Inquiry

### Nested Research Sub-Questions

2. **Technological Intervention**: What design elements make Google Form-based ERs effective for remote learning?

## üí° Synthesized Key Insights
Virtual ERs, built on Google Forms, proved effective for engagement and socialization during the COVID-19 shift to online learning. Case 1 (entrepreneurial) used competition and festivity for levity, while Case 2 (anatomy) leveraged narrative for optional review. Recommendations include small teams (2-5), 30-minute duration, and hints to reduce frustration (p. 42-43). Lack of student data limits claims, but educator reflections suggest scalability and hybrid potential (p. 45).
