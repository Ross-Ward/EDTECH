## üí° Synthesized Key Insights

The study reveals a proactive yet cautious approach to GenAI in U.S. higher education, with four dominant topics: learning integration, multimodal applications, security/ethics, and academic integrity (#ResearchFindings). Sentiment is overwhelmingly positive, especially for faculty (#SentimentAnalysis), though student guidelines are more regulatory (#StakeholderDifferences). Qualitative analysis highlights the need for flexible, stakeholder-specific policies amid rapid technological change (#PolicyDesign).
