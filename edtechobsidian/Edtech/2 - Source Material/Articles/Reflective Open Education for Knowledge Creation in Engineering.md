## üí° Synthesized Key Insights

Habash‚Äôs pedagogy shifts from knowledge acquisition to creation using an "acquire-reflect-share-apply" framework. Implemented in ELG4125 (smart grid CBL/PBL) and ELG3336 (mechatronics LBD), it leverages OERs (g9toengineering.com, greenengineers.ca) for sharing. Peer evaluation and questionnaires (60% strongly agree) confirm reflective tasks enhance creativity, though 7% find them demanding. Digital tools amplify engagement but require skill acquisition.
