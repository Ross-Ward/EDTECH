# Can Generative Artificial Intelligence Write an Academic Journal Article?

ID: 20240325-GENAI-ACADEMIC-WRITING

Source: Hsu, H. P. (2023). Can Generative Artificial Intelligence Write an Academic Journal Article? Irish Journal of Technology Enhanced Learning, 7(2), 158-171.

Tags: #GenerativeAI #AcademicWriting #ChatGPT #ResearchMethodology #HigherEducation

Date: 2023-07-25

## Summary

Hsiao-Ping Hsu explores the potential and limitations of using Generative AI (specifically ChatGPT 4.0) to write an academic journal article. The study documents a detailed process of using AI for manuscript generation, highlighting both the opportunities and challenges in academic writing and research design.

## Key Findings

1. Gen AI Capabilities
    - Efficient in proofreading and editing
    - Assists in research idea generation
    - Provides structured content generation
    - Simulates two-way communication for language learning
2. Challenges in AI-Assisted Writing
    - Tendency to omit critical research variables
    - Inconsistent response generation
    - Potential for logically incorrect results
    - Unreliable citation management
3. Methodological Insights
    - Used three-stage process: brainstorming, refinement, writing
    - Employed prompt engineering techniques
    - Critically evaluated AI-generated content
    - Demonstrated iterative design approach
