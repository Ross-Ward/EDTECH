## Summary
This review explores AI-driven personalized learning paths, synthesizing literature to show enhanced performance, engagement, and retention. Grounded in cognitive psychology, it highlights adaptability and inclusivity, while noting ethical challenges like privacy and bias.

## Key Findings
- **Effectiveness:** AI improves understanding, retention.
- **Adaptability:** Matches diverse styles.
- **Engagement:** Boosts motivation.
- **Inclusivity:** Reduces disparities.

## Methodology
- **Design:** Systematic review.
- **Sources:** PubMed, IEEE Xplore, ERIC, Google Scholar (last 10 years).
- **Criteria:** Peer-reviewed AI studies in education.

## Concepts
