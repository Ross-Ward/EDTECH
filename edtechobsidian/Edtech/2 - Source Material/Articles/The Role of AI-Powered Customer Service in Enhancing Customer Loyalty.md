## üéØ Multidimensional Research Inquiry

### Core Research Question
How does AI-powered customer service influence customer loyalty through the mediating effects of customer satisfaction and perceived efficiency?

### Nested Research Sub-Questions
1. **Cognitive Dimension**: How do customers perceive the efficiency of AI-driven interactions in shaping their satisfaction?
2. **Technological Intervention**: What specific AI capabilities (e.g., chatbots, NLP) enhance perceived efficiency and satisfaction?
3. **Learning Ecosystem Impact**: How does AI adoption in customer service transform organizational relationships with customers?
