Title: zettelkasten method

Based on using a [[zettelkasten]] and linking [[zettel]]s together.

The Zettelkasten method is an amplifier of your endeavors in the realm of knowledge work. It's a personal tool for thinking and writing and has a hypertextual feature to make a web of thought possible. The difference to other systems is that you create a web of thoughts instead of notes of arbitrary size and form, and emphasize connection, not a collection.
