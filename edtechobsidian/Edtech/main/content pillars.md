Title: content pillars

A content pillar is a subject that that can be broken into many derivative sections, topics, subtopics, and content. It acts as a unifying and consistent way of coming up with new ideas based around the subject.

Applied with the concept of idea emergence, content can be created from the master note or related MOCs.

**Basic Content Pillar Layout:**
1. Subject
	1. Topic 1
		1. Subtopic
			1. Content
			2. Content
			3. Content
		2. Subtopic
	2. Topic 2
		1. Subtopic
			1. Content
	3. Topic 3
