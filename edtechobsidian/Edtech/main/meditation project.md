## Vision
In three sentences or less, write your what and your why for the project:

Using [[meditation]] to bring emotionally stability for my mental health. It doesn't require anything fancy and can be trained and developed. There is no goal in meditation, meditation simply is an on-going process.

**Goal:** Make meditation a lifelong process.

## Medium-term Goals (3-6 months)
- [ ] Meditate for 10 minutes daily within 3 months
- [ ] Meditate forr 20-30 minutes within 6 months
- [ ] Have a filled up log of meditation observations

---
## Daily Tasks
- [ ] Meditate 3-5 minutes
	- [ ] Use guided meditations if struggling in the beginning (eventually, goal is to not need anything to meditate anywhere)
- [ ] Keep a log of meditation sessions and write down how you feel after
