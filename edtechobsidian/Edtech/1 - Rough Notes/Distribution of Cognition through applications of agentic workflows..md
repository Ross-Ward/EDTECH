**Abstract**

This research examines how Large language models(LLM)- Augment Low-Code/No-Code (LCNC) environments function as distributed cognitive systems( Edwin Hutchins,1995) that fundamentally reshape the process of development for for novice and intermediate learners. From a theoretical lens of distributed cognition, this study which represents a transformation in human-LLM-LCNC assemblage as a single cognitive unit which propagates knowledge to jointly acomplish tasks for the learner through visual interfaces and underlying AI agents.
