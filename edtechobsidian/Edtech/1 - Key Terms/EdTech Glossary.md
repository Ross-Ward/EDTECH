## A
### Adaptive Learning
A technology-based educational system that dynamically adjusts to learner needs and performance. [[Adaptive Learning Systems]]

### AI in Education (AIEd)
The application of artificial intelligence technologies to enhance teaching and learning processes. [[AI in Education Index]]

### Asynchronous Learning
Learning that occurs at different times for different students, allowing flexibility in scheduling and pace. [[Digital Learning Strategy]]

## B
### Blended Learning
An approach combining face-to-face instruction with online learning activities. [[Blended Learning Guide]]

### BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)
Policy allowing students to use personal devices for learning activities. [[Mobile Learning Technologies]]

## C
### Collaborative Learning
Educational approach where students work together in groups to solve problems or complete tasks. [[Student Engagement Guide]]

### Competency-Based Learning
Learning model where students advance based on demonstrated mastery of skills and knowledge. [[Assessment Methods Index]]

## D
### Digital Literacy
The ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies. [[Digital Learning Strategy]]

### Data Analytics in Education
The collection and analysis of educational data to improve teaching and learning. [[Learning Analytics]]

## E
### E-Learning
Education that takes place through digital devices and internet-based platforms. [[Digital Learning Strategy]]

### Engagement Analytics
Metrics and data analysis focused on student participation and interaction with learning materials. [[Student Engagement Guide]]

## F
### Flipped Learning
Instructional strategy where traditional learning is "flipped" - content delivery occurs outside class, while practice occurs in class. [[Blended Learning Guide]]

### Formative Assessment
Ongoing assessment during learning to provide immediate feedback and adjust instruction. [[Assessment Methods Index]]

## G
### Gamification
The application of game-design elements in educational contexts. [[Gamification in Education]]

## H
### Hybrid Learning
Educational model combining online and in-person learning experiences. [[Blended Learning Guide]]

## I
### Instructional Design
Systematic development of educational experiences using learning and pedagogical theory. [[Educational Technology Framework]]

### Interactive Learning
Learning experiences that engage students through active participation and response. [[Student Engagement Guide]]

## L
### Learning Analytics
The measurement, collection, analysis, and reporting of data about learners and their contexts. [[Learning Analytics]]

### Learning Management System (LMS)
Software platform for delivering educational courses and training programs. [[Digital Learning Strategy]]

## M
### Microlearning
Learning strategy delivering content in small, specific bursts. [[Mobile Learning Technologies]]

### Mixed Reality (MR)
Combination of real and virtual worlds to create new environments for learning. [[Extended Reality in Education]]

## O
### Online Learning
Education that takes place over the internet. [[Digital Learning Strategy]]

## P
### Personalized Learning
Tailoring education to individual student needs, goals, and abilities. [[AI in Education Index]]

### Project-Based Learning (PBL)
Teaching method where students learn through active exploration of real-world challenges. [[Student Engagement Guide]]

## S
### Synchronous Learning
Learning that occurs in real-time, with all participants present simultaneously. [[Digital Learning Strategy]]

### Student Information System (SIS)
Software managing student data and academic records. [[Educational Technology Framework]]

## T
### Technology Integration
The use of technology tools in teaching and learning. [[Educational Technology Framework]]

## V
### Virtual Learning Environment (VLE)
Online system for delivering learning materials to students. [[Digital Learning Strategy]]

### Virtual Reality (VR)
Computer-generated simulation of three-dimensional learning environments. [[Extended Reality in Education]]
